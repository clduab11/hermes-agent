## üéØ Pricing Psychology & Strategy

### Why $499/month for Professional?

1. **Anchoring:** Just under $500 feels significantly cheaper than $500+
2. **Value perception:** $499/month = $16.63/day = less than one billable hour
3. **Competitor comparison:**
   - Virtual receptionist services: $500-$2,000/month (limited hours, per-minute billing)
   - Full-time receptionist: $3,000-$5,000/month (salary + benefits)
   - HERMES at $499 = 90% cheaper than receptionist, comparable to virtual service but 24/7

4. **ROI justification:** One additional case pays for 2+ years of service

### Why $1,999/month for Enterprise?

1. **4X pricing ladder:** Psychological multiplier (2x would be too close, 5x too far)
2. **Value alignment:** Multi-location firms save $100K+ vs. traditional staffing
3. **Dedicated support justification:** Success manager adds $500-$1,000 in perceived value
4. **Enterprise expectations:** Firms expect to pay $1,500-$3,000 for "enterprise" software

### Annual Discount Strategy

**20% discount (2 months free) when paid annually**

**Benefits:**
- **Cash flow:** Collect 10 months of revenue upfront
- **Lower churn:** Annual customers are stickier (87% renewal vs. 60% monthly)
- **Predictable revenue:** Easier to forecast and plan
- **Sales efficiency:** Larger deal sizes mean fewer transactions

**Expected uptake:** 40% of customers choose annual

## üîÑ Pricing Optimization Strategy

### Month 1-3: Beta Pricing (Validation)

**Offer:**
- Professional: $299/month (40% discount) for first 3 customers
- Goal: Validate product-market fit, not maximize revenue
- Collect feedback for pricing perception

### Month 4-6: Early Adopter Pricing

**Offer:**
- Professional: $399/month (20% discount) for next 10 customers
- Lock in rate for 12 months, then increase to $499
- Create urgency: "Price increases to $499 on [date]"

### Month 7+: Standard Pricing

**Offer:**
- Professional: $499/month (standard)
- Enterprise: $1,999/month (standard)
- Occasional promotions (e.g., "$299 first month" for trials)

### Annual Price Increases

**Strategy:** Increase prices 5-10% annually for new customers
- Grandfather existing customers at current rate (builds loyalty)
- Communicate price increases 60 days in advance for monthly customers
- Justify with new features and improvements
